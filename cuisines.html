Delicious Filipino Cuisines

Adobo

A popular Filipino dish made of meat marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and spices.

Sinigang

A sour soup made from tamarind, tomatoes, and various meats or seafood.

Lechon

Roasted whole pig, famous for its crispy skin and tender meat.

Pancit

Stir-fried noodles with various vegetables and meats.

Kare-Kare

A traditional Filipino stew made with oxtail, tripe, and a rich peanut sauce.
